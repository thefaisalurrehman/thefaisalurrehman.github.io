2. Description of Service

Roomy is an Android app that helps you clean up your photo and video gallery to free up storage. The app provides:

  • Scanning your gallery to identify photos, videos, screenshots, duplicates, similar photos, and large files
  • Reviewing media using a fast swipe interface — delete, keep, or set aside for later
  • Automatic detection of duplicate and similar photos using on-device image fingerprints
  • Reviewing your selections before confirming deletion
  • Deleting media via Android's system delete — move to Trash (recoverable for 30 days) or permanent delete
  • Tracking storage freed and files cleaned

5. Deletion of Your Media — Important

You are in control of every deletion. Roomy never deletes media without your confirmation. When you choose to delete, Android's own system dialog asks you to confirm. You may choose to move files to Trash (recoverable for 30 days through your gallery) or to delete permanently.
  • Permanent deletion cannot be undone. If you choose permanent delete, those photos and videos are removed from your device and cannot be recovered by Roomy.
  • Trash items follow Android's rules. Items moved to Trash are managed by Android and are typically recoverable for 30 days, after which the system removes them.
  • Back up important media first. We strongly recommend backing up any media you value before cleaning. Roomy is not responsible for media you choose to delete.
