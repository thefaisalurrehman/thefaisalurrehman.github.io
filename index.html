About

Independent. Focused.

TenDegree is a small, independent Android studio. We build a handful of apps and try to make each one genuinely good — fast, clean, and respectful of the people who use them.

Our apps are built with modern Android: Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, MVVM, and clean architecture. We care about smooth performance, thoughtful design, and privacy by default. No selling your data, no dark patterns, no clutter.

Roomy is our first app — and we're just getting started.
